Privacy Policy for Learn AI

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Learn AI mobile application (the "Service") provided by bigberri ("we", "us", or "our").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide to us directly: For account creation and management, we use a third-party service, Clerk. Through Clerk, we may collect your name, email address, and password. This information is used to create and secure your account.
  • Information we collect automatically: When you use the Service, we automatically collect information about your activity, such as your lesson progress and quiz results. This is used to save your progress and personalize your learning experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Service.
  • Allow you to create an account and save your learning progress.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Service.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our Service.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the Service.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers that help us operate our Service. These include:

  • Clerk: For user authentication and account management. You can view their privacy policy here: https://clerk.com/privacy
  • Expo: To build and run the application. You can view their privacy policy here: https://expo.dev/privacy

4. Online Payments

We do not accept or process any payments online. The Learn AI application is free to use, and does not contain any features for purchases or financial transactions. Therefore, we do not collect any payment or credit card information, and we do not use any payment vendors.

5. Account Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. To do so, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at deletion.request@bigberri.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, please include the email address associated with your Learn AI account so we can identify it.

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your account and all personal data associated with it, including your name, email address, and learning progress. This action cannot be undone. We do not retain any personal data after deletion.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or until you request its deletion.

7. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use third-party services like Clerk that follow industry best practices for security, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us
